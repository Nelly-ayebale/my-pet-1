SNOW

My cat is named Snow. This is her name because she is white with only a black patch on her head.

I got her when she was just a kitten and within two years, she has grown to be a healthy grown cat.

Snow lies in the sun all day and enjoys taking naps in the couch.She enjoys chasing after balls of yawn and also chasing mice around the house.

She recently gave birth to two kittens where one was given away. However Snow still showed her kitten all the maternal attention a mother could give.

When she was a kitten, she loved to pounce around a small green ball and also chase small insects but now she rarely even gives the ball attention.

However, she still chews up on insects

Snow loves to eat and respects all her meal times. She usually comes running when I call her for food.

With keen attention to the food I give her she loves Chicken compared to other meats.

I usually give her the following food:

  • Chicken
  • Beef
  • Avocado
  • Canned cat food

    • However, she rarely eats the Avocadoes.
